Preliminary for Schools

Compact Preliminary for Schools is a concise and focused course which thoroughly prepares B1-level students for Cambridge English: Preliminary for Schools. The course is designed to maximise the performance of school-age learners, and covers the core Preliminary for Schools topics, language and skills.

Autores
Caroline Nixon, Michael Tomlinson


Level
A1-A2 (Complete Beginner to Elementary)


Informed by the Cambridge English Corpus
Learn More


Key Features

  • The new 'My Home Booklet' revises vocabulary and grammar and helps self-assess basic competences development at home with parents.

  • Phonics sections help improve pronunciation, spelling and reading skills, while the 'Say it with me' sections focus on common pronunciation problems for Spanish speakers.

  • An improved focus on grammar, with Grammar boxes and a Grammar reference in the Pupil's Book, plus a Grammar practice in the Activity Book.

  • New resources to exploit stories in the classroom: character masks, story summaries, and playscripts.

  • Exam-type tests in levels 2, 4 and 6 help to assess the children's progress, and familiarise them with the format of the Cambridge English: Young Learners exam.
