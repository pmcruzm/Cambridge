Cambridge Labs

LABS

A space for ideas about English Language Teaching where you are the star.

Subir
  • Teacher Development
  • Cambridge Labs
  • Cambridge English for Schools
  • English for Spanish Speakers
  • Events

Cambridge Labs es un espacio para la generación de ideas sobre la enseñanza del inglés. Un espacio pensado para la participación y la cocreación entre nosotros (Cambridge University Press) y profesores que imparten inglés, en cualquier nivel y tipo de centro y en todo el territorio nacional.

Desarrollamos diferentes tipos de actividad de investigación, desde workshops creativos hasta encuestas, y siempre de manera incentivada.

¿Te apetece ?

Focus Groups

We often conduct paid focus groups with english teachers. Your opinion will help us improve our existing products and develop new ones. Without your feedback, our work doesn't have meaning.

Contests

We frequently organise raffles and contests on our Facebook page and other specific websitesfor both English teachers and students.

Ideas Lab

At Cambridge University Press, we believe in the power of ideas. We always keep a channel of communication open with a group of English teachers in order to generate new ideas, undertake new ELT projects and test methods. Are you up for it?.

Be in the know

Be among the first to hear about our new product launches, get discounts, meet our authors, and participate in events and training courses.
